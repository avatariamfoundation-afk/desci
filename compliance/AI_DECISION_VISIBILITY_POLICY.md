# AI_DECISION_VISIBILITY_POLICY.md  
**Policy for Transparency, Explainability, and Oversight of AI-Influenced Decisions**

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the **mandatory visibility requirements** for all AI-influenced decisions within the NeuroGrid ecosystem.

Its purpose is to ensure that:
- AI outputs are never opaque
- Human users understand when AI is involved
- Decision pathways are inspectable and auditable
- Accountability is preserved at all times

Invisible AI influence is prohibited.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all
